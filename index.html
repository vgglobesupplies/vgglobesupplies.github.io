About VG Globe Supplies

VG Globe Supplies is a premier supplier of high-end medical and accessibility equipment. We provide everything necessary for enhanced living, specializing in advanced wheelchairs, automatic massage beds, and a comprehensive range of luxury medical products designed for superior comfort and independence.

Our mission is to improve the quality of life for individuals with disabilities by offering premium products that combine cutting-edge technology with exceptional comfort and durability.

With years of experience in the medical supply industry, our team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect solutions for your specific needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of payment do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and we also offer financing options for qualifying customers on purchases over $1,000. For institutional purchases, we can arrange invoice billing.

Do you offer installation services for your equipment?

+

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our premium equipment. Our certified technicians will deliver, set up, and demonstrate how to use your equipment properly. Installation fees vary based on the complexity of the equipment.

What is your warranty policy?

+

All our products come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the equipment. Additionally, we offer extended warranty options for most products. Our team will provide complete warranty documentation with your purchase.

Do you work with insurance companies?

+

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers and Medicare/Medicaid. Our team can help you navigate the insurance process and provide necessary documentation. Please contact us with your insurance information so we can verify coverage.

What is your delivery timeframe?

+

Standard delivery takes 10-17 business days within California and 22-25 business days nationwide. For urgent needs, we offer expedited shipping options. Custom or specialized equipment may require additional time for delivery.

Do you offer rental options for equipment?

+

Yes, we offer short-term and long-term rental options for many of our products. This is ideal for temporary needs or for customers who want to try equipment before purchasing. Contact us for rental availability and pricing.

  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

+

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your transactions and send related information
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Communicate with you about products, services, and promotional offers
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services

Information Sharing

+

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • In connection with business transfers (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
